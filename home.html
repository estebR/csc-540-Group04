Database‑Driven Web App

Organize campus tutoring with availability‑aware scheduling

Students can find and book tutors across subjects, while admins manage tutors, rooms, and schedules. The system enforces capacity and eligibility rules and prevents double‑booking by design.

  • Student experience: log in, search availability, book/cancel sessions.
  • Admin console: manage tutors, rooms, subjects, schedules.
  • Safety checks: conflicts, capacity, and eligibility are validated automatically.
  • Notifications: email confirmations and updates keep everyone aligned.

Description

The University Tutoring Management System streamlines tutoring operations with a centralized database and clean workflows. It supports student self‑service scheduling and robust administrative controls, ensuring data accuracy and operational reliability.

Motivation

Inspired by SCSU’s Center for Academic Success and Accessibility Services (CASAS), this project focuses on improving flexibility, preventing double‑booking, and strengthening data integrity in campus tutoring programs.

Supported Features

Student Self‑Service

Search tutors by subject and time, then book or cancel sessions with instant validation.

Conflict Prevention

Automatic checks eliminate double‑booking and enforce room capacity and eligibility rules.

Administrative Control

Maintain tutor profiles, room inventories, subject catalogs, and recurring schedules.

Notifications

Send confirmations and updates so students and tutors always have the latest details.
